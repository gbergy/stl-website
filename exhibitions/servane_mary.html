July 5 - July 28, 2012
Artist Reception: July 5, 7 - 9 pm

Shoot TheShoot The Lobster is pleased to announce a show of selected works by Servane Mary at Clint Roenisch Gallery. Mary’s prints of defamed actresses and singers on silk evoke the complicated private/social faces of notoriety.

An Artist Reception will inaugurate the show on Thursday, July 5 at 7pm.
