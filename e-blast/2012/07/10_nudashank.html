Artist Reception: July 10, 6 - 8 pm

Shoot The Lobster – New York City is pleased to announce a collaboration with Nudashank, an independent, artist-run gallery in Baltimore, Maryland founded by Seth Adelsberger and Alex Ebstein. Opened in 2009, Nudashank focuses on showcasing and contextualizing Baltimore artists with emerging artists nationally and internationally.

This exhibition brings together three artists who each approach their work with a distinct material sensibility that recombines easily recognized urban construction and textural elements in direct ways. These works exemplify a raw exploration of abstraction and materiality through photography, painting, and sculpture.

An Artists Reception will be held Tuesday, July 10, 6 - 8 pm.
